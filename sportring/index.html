SportRing

SportRing is an activity management app for Apple Watch that intuitively visualizes your workout distances—Walking, Running, and Cycling—using "Rings." Set your monthly goals and check your daily achievement at a glance. Its clean, minimal design provides effortless support in building your workout habit. Supported languages: English, Japanese, French, German, Spanish, Chinese (Simplified/Traditional), Korean, and Thai.

Check Your Progress Anytime on Apple Watch

Check your monthly goals and progress anytime on your wrist.

  • ・ Automatic sync for Walking, Running, Cycling, Swimming, Hiking, Skiing, and Rowing distances
  • NEWNow supporting Wheelchair and Hand Cycling! (May 6, 2026)
  • ・ Beautiful Ring animations tracking your progress
  • ・ View your daily steps and 'Walking + Running'
  • ・ Adjust goal distance, ring colors, and km/mi units directly on the standalone Watch app
  • ・ Rings update instantly after each workout
  • ・ Supports 4 types of Apple Watch complications. Quick progress checks and app launch with a tap

Workout History & Monthly Graphs

The iPhone app provides a detailed list of your workouts, allowing you to review your progress alongside beautiful monthly graphs. With iPhone widget support, you can also track your progress right from your Home Screen.

  • ・ Detailed lists for smart workout history management
  • ・ Daily activity visualized with clear bar graphs
  • ・ iPhone widget support to view your progress at a glance

Personalized Settings

Customize the app to match your style and preferences for a truly personal experience. You can also choose your display language from English, Japanese, French, German, Spanish, Chinese (Simplified/Traditional), Korean, and Thai.

  • ・ Fully customizable Ring colors for each workout
  • ・ Set goal distances and km/mi units for each workout in one place
  • ・ Use checkboxes to show or hide workouts
  • ・ Easily reorder workouts by dragging the handles on the right
  • ・ Select your preferred display language via iPhone Settings → Apps → SportRing → Preferred Language

Full iPad Support

View each workout at a glance with a large-screen layout.

  • ・ View Rings and monthly graphs side-by-side
  • ・ Smooth navigation with the sidebar

【How to Use】

  1. Select your workout (Walking, Running, Cycling, Swimming, Hiking, Skiing, Rowing, Wheelchair, or Hand Cycling)
  2. Set your monthly distance goals
  3. Exercise with Apple Watch to sync automatically
  4. Check daily activity on the monthly graph
  5. Customize your ring colors
  6. View your daily steps and 'Walking + Running' alongside your other activities

【Best For】

  • 🏃 Looking to build a consistent workout habit
  • 🎯 Anyone seeking simple, monthly goal tracking
  • ⌚ Apple Watch owners who want better data visualization
  • ✨ Those who value simplicity and ease of use
