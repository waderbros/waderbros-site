Behind the Scenes

Making Your Progress More Personal.

I love being active, but I always felt the standard Apple Watch rings left something to be desired for specific goals.

"How many kilometers left for my monthly run?"
"How much did I cycle this week?"

I wanted to feel my progress directly, through that iconic, beautiful ring format.

That’s why I created SportRing. Whether it’s hiking, skiing, or rowing, I wanted to seamlessly track the specific sports I’m passionate about.

Combining the elegance of a native Apple app with the deep customization an athlete needs—that is the heart behind this app.
