Privacy Policy

1. Basic Policy

SportRing (“the App”) is an activity tracking app for Apple Watch and iPhone.
We respect your privacy and are committed to handling personal and health-related data safely and responsibly.

2. Data Collected

The App uses Apple’s HealthKit framework to access:

  • Walking and running distance data
  • Activity statistics

This data is used only for displaying your progress as rings and graphs within the app.

3. Data Storage and Sharing

All collected data is stored securely on your device.
No data is transmitted to, or shared with, any third parties.

4. Use of HealthKit

SportRing integrates with Apple’s HealthKit to read and display activity data.
HealthKit data is managed in compliance with Apple’s guidelines and is never used for other purposes without explicit user consent.

5. Advertising and Analytics

The App does not currently include ads or third-party analytics tools.
If these features are added in the future, users will be informed in advance.

6. About Paid Version and Data Handling

The App is currently available for free as a beta version.
We may consider a paid version in the future, but this will not affect how your data is handled — your privacy will remain protected.
