Privacy Policy

SportRing respects your privacy and does not collect personal data without consent.

Data Collection

All workout and activity data is stored locally on your device and synchronized through Apple HealthKit. No data is transmitted or stored on cloud services or servers.

Third-party Services

SportRing does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking tools. All permissions (e.g., HealthKit access) are user-controlled.
