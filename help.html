Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How do I install SportRing?
A: Search "SportRing" on the App Store and install it on your iPhone. The Watch app will automatically install on your Apple Watch.

Q2: My ring data is not updating.
A: Ensure Bluetooth is enabled and both devices are nearby. If the problem persists, restart your Apple Watch and iPhone.

Q3: How can I contact support?
A: Please email us at contact@waderbros.com with details of your issue.
